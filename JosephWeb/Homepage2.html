Labrador Retriever

Breed Name: Labrador Retriever

Origin: Canada

Overview: Labs are friendly, outgoing, and high-spirited companions who have more than enough affection to go around.

Size: 25-36 Kg

Height: 21-24 inches

History: Labrador Retrievers were bred to be both a friendly companion and a useful working dog breed, particularly for retrieving fish and game.

German Shepherd

Breed Name: German Shepherd

Origin: Germany

Overview: German Shepherds are confident, courageous, and smart. They are strong and versatile working dogs.

Size: 30-40 Kg

Height: 22-26 inches

History: Originally bred for herding sheep, German Shepherds became one of the most popular working dogs due to their intelligence and versatility.

Golden Retriever

Breed Name: Golden Retriever

Origin: Scotland

Overview: Known for their friendly, tolerant attitude, Golden Retrievers are great family pets.

Size: 25-34 Kg

Height: 20-24 inches

History: Golden Retrievers were originally bred for retrieving game, particularly waterfowl during hunting and shooting parties.

Beagle

Breed Name: Beagle

Origin: United Kingdom

Overview: Beagles are curious, friendly, and great with families, known for their excellent tracking ability.

Size: 9-14 Kg

Height: 13-15 inches

History: Beagles were bred primarily for hunting hare, and their incredible sense of smell makes them great scent hounds.

Poodle

Breed Name: Poodle

Origin: Germany

Overview: Poodles are intelligent, energetic, and have hypoallergenic coats, making them great pets for allergy sufferers.

Size: 20-30 Kg

Height: 15-24 inches

History: Originally bred as water retrievers, Poodles are known for their intelligence and versatility . Poodles can perform various tasks and are known for their agility and obedience.

Bulldog

Breed Name: Bulldog

Origin: England

Overview: Bulldogs are gentle, affectionate, and known for their loose, wrinkled skin and distinctive pushed-in nose.

Size: 23-25 Kg

Height: 14-15 inches

History: Bulldogs were originally bred for bull-baiting, but now they are beloved family pets known for their loyalty.

Dachshund

Breed Name: Dachshund

Origin: Germany

Overview: Dachshunds are playful, curious, and known for their long bodies and short legs, making them distinctive.

Size: 7-15 Kg

Height: 8-9 inches

History: Originally bred to hunt badgers, Dachshunds are now popular pets known for their unique appearance and playful nature.

Rottweiler

Breed Name: Rottweiler

Origin: Germany

Overview: Rottweilers are confident, fearless, and great companions, known for their loyalty and strength.

Size: 35-60 Kg

Height: 22-27 inches

History: Rottweilers were originally bred as herding and guarding dogs, and they excel in protection and obedience work.

Boxer

Breed Name: Boxer

Origin: Germany

Overview: Boxers are playful, energetic, and known for their strong build and boundless enthusiasm.

Size: 25-32 Kg

Height: 21-25 inches

History: Boxers were originally bred for hunting and guarding, and they make excellent family pets due to their affectionate nature.
